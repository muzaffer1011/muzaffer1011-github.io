Frequently asked questions



Is Lancelot only for Ex-consultant and ex-developers?

No. Any independent professional can register and establish reputation at Lancelot.

How does Lancelot preset an opportunity to me?

We are ahead of the curve. We’ve been diligently working behind the scenes and have approached 500+ potential clients. They will access Lancelot to connect with high performing contractors like yourself. Your profile will be shared with them, and clients who are impressed with your outstanding qualifications will request an interview.

how does Lancelot's job search process differ from other online platforms?

First and foremost - it’s a dynamic time saver. You can skip the hassle of searching, applying, and bidding for jobs. At Lancelot, your personal reputation based on your project performance will actively conduct searches that will match someone of your caliber with a premier potential client. Without you lifting a finger, your chance to obtain high-quality jobs increases exponentially.

Does my profile and all related scores automatically become public or will I be able to select who will be able to view it? Will I be able to control this?

First, please be assured that it will not be made public. Lancelot consists of numerous closed micro-communities of project managers. Your profile will be shared and promoted only among your actual previous clients (reviewers) and their 1st and 2nd tier connections. Each one trusts the other’s contractor reviews. On the other hand, you may share your own profile including your reputation data with anyone you’d like.

Can I see how broadly my reputation has been shared?

Absolutely. Lancelot has developed a “Reputation Amplification Score,” which you can view from your profile summary. The score will be calculated from factors including the number of views and the magnitude of their connections.

Suppose I recieve a poor review and believe it wasn't fair, How long will it be attached to my profile?

We have adopted two ways to proactively address this issue. First, our algorithm will review outlier scores and exclude them from your profile. Second, we have developed a “Fairness Score,” which will measure each reviewer’s fairness in their reviews, and apply different weights on them when averaging the final score. Additionally, you have the option to de-list your profile and reputation data from Lancelot at any time.

How long must I wait until i can view my client's feedback?

You will gain access to your first reputation report after you receive your first three reviews. The next profile update is activated at the completion of three additional reviews. In essence, your reputation will be updated once each batch (three reviews) is completed.

Is it only my previous client who can provide feedback?

No. Your project managers and colleagues at the previous projects, your end-clients (client of consulting firms), and individuals from 3rd party agencies who worked closely with you can also endorse you. But the impact on your reputation will vary according to their working relationship with you.

Can I see an individual review and know who submitted it?

No. This is confidential information. All reviews and ratings will be consolidated into a single report with reviewer names censored. This confidentiality allows reviewers to write objective, unbiased reviews.

Is self-assesstment mandatory?

Yes, if you want to see feedback from your reviewers. Your self-assessment for each project will also help us identify outlier reviews. Comparison between self-assessment and reviews from your clients will offer you greater opportunities to understand your professional strengths and weaknesses.

Does my self-assessment report become public? Who will have access to it?

No. It will only be available to you.
